Our Founder

Welcome to SG Pani Puri! My name is Shekhar Gorakh Ghorwade, and I'm proud to be the second generation in our family to carry forward the legacy of this beloved business. I completed my B.E. in Computer Sciences from Pune, and after a fulfilling academic journey, I decided to join our family business in 2023.
The roots of SG Pani Puri go back to 2010 when my mom, Surekha, started this venture with a simple yet powerful goal: to bring the authentic taste of Pani Puri to the people of Pune. Over the past 12 years, my mom has dedicated herself to perfecting every aspect of our offerings, from the crispness of the puris to the tanginess of the tamarind water, ensuring that each bite delivers a burst of flavor and joy.
